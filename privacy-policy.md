# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026

Hleb Siarhei values your privacy and is dedicated to ensuring the security of any information collected from or about you. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle Personal Information obtained when you interact with our website, applications, and services, including the AI Antique Identifier - Vintage Appraiser & Value Scan app (collectively, the "Services").

## 1. Personal Information We Collect

### Primary Account Information

To enhance your experience and facilitate communication, AI Antique Identifier - Vintage Appraiser & Value Scan collects primary account details. This information is solely used for sending notifications, updates, and essential information related to our services. Your primary account data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent and is not utilized for purposes beyond improving your app experience.

### Information You Provide

When you use our Services, create an account, or contact us, we collect the following:

- **Account Information:** Includes your name, contact details, account credentials, payment information, and transaction history.
- **User Content:** Photos of items you submit for appraisal, along with any other inputs, files, or feedback you provide via our Services. Photos are processed to identify the item and estimate its age, origin, and value.
- **Communication Data:** Details from messages you send us, including your name, contact information, and social media interactions.

### Automatically Collected Information

When you interact with our Services, we automatically collect:

- **Log Data:** IP address, browser details, request timestamps, and interactions.
- **Usage Data:** Engagement metrics such as viewed content, accessed features, and session details.
- **Device Information:** Includes device name, operating system, and identifiers.
- **Cookies and Analytics:** Cookies are used to manage your sessions and enhance your experience. Analytics tools help us better understand user behavior and improve our services.

## 2. How We Use Personal Information

We use Personal Information for purposes such as:

- Operating and improving our Services, including AI-based identification and value estimation.
- Communicating updates and support.
- Ensuring security and fraud prevention.
- Conducting research and developing new features.
- Complying with legal obligations and protecting rights.

Aggregated or de-identified data may be used for analytical purposes and service improvement.

## 3. Sharing Personal Information

We may share your information in the following cases:
